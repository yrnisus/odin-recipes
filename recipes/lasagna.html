Ingredients

Steps


  1. Make the sauce
  2. Prepare the noodles and cheeses
  3. Layer sauce on the bottom of the pan.
  4. Top with noodles.
  5. Top with ricotta cheese mixture.
  6. Sprinkle on mozzarella cheese.
  7. Sprinkle on Parmesan cheese.
  8. Add more sauce.
  9. Repeat layers starting with another layer of noodles.
  10. Spread on the rest of the ricotta.
  11. Sprinkle on more mozzarella.
  12. Add more Parmesan
  13. Layer on more sauce.
  14. A final top layer of noodles, sauce, and the rest of the Parmesan and Mozzarella cheese.
